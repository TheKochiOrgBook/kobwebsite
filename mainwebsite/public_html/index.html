KochiOrgBook

KochiOrgBook(KOB) gives ownership of all your digital Identities back to you the real owners. KOB aims to connects Citizens of Kochi to a Network of Verifiable Associations who issue credentials and proofs that can by used to avail various services in the city. These proofs that can be presented only with the consent of the owners of the credentials.
